Safety Guidelines

Before the Ride

  • Verify passenger details and ratings
  • Conduct pre-trip vehicle inspection
  • Plan and review your route
  • Share trip details with trusted contacts
  • Ensure your phone is fully charged

During the Ride

  • Strictly follow traffic rules and speed limits
  • Stay alert and avoid distractions
  • Maintain professional behavior and boundaries
  • Keep emergency contacts easily accessible
  • Use in-app navigation for optimal routes

After the Ride

  • Report any safety concerns immediately
  • Provide honest passenger ratings
  • Document any incidents with details
  • Check vehicle condition

Vehicle Safety

Regular Maintenance

  • Schedule regular oil changes and fluid checks
  • Monitor tire pressure and tread depth
  • Regular brake system inspections
  • Check all lights and signals
  • Maintain clean windshield and mirrors

Required Safety Equipment

  • First aid kit (fully stocked)
  • Fire extinguisher (ABC type)
  • Emergency warning triangle
  • Spare tire and jack
  • Basic tool kit

Emergency Procedures

In Case of Emergency

  1. Use the emergency button in the RideX app
  2. Contact local emergency services (911) if needed
  3. Report the incident to RideX support team
  4. Document the situation with photos if safe to do so
  5. Follow instructions from emergency responders
