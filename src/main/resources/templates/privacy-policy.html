Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Carpool's Privacy Policy. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our carpooling service. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name and contact information
  • Email address and phone number
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Payment information
  • Driver's license and vehicle information (for drivers)

2.2 Location Data

We collect location data to provide our carpooling services, including:

  • Pick-up and drop-off locations
  • Real-time location during rides
  • Travel routes and destinations

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our carpooling services
  • Match drivers with passengers
  • Process payments and transactions
  • Send important notifications and updates
  • Ensure safety and security
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Other users (limited information for ride matching)
  • Service providers and business partners
  • Law enforcement when required by law

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure servers and networks
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience and collect usage data. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
