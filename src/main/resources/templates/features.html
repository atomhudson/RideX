How We Change the Game

RideX is packed with features designed to make carpooling safe, seamless, and reliable for everyone.

Government ID Verification

Verify users with valid government-issued IDs to ensure platform authenticity and security for every ride.

Car Registration

Drivers register their vehicles with full details and documents for complete transparency and trust.

Phone Verification

OTP-based phone verification ensures every user is reachable and accountable on the platform.

Ride Publishing

Drivers can publish rides with origin, destination, date, time, and available seats in a streamlined flow.

Smart Ride Search

Search for rides by city, date, and preferences with intelligent filters to find the perfect match.

Route Visualization

Interactive maps powered by Leaflet display the complete route between origin and destination for every ride.

Rating System

Rate drivers and passengers after each ride to build trust and maintain community standards.

Ride Requests

Passengers can request to join rides, and drivers can accept or decline based on their preferences.

Reward Coins

Earn coins for publishing rides, joining trips, and building your reputation on the platform.

Secure Authentication

Sign in with Google or GitHub OAuth alongside traditional email login for maximum security and convenience.

Detailed Profiles

Complete user profiles with bio, occupation, age, city, and verification badges for trust and transparency.

System Monitoring

Prometheus and Grafana integration enables real-time performance tracking and system reliability monitoring.
