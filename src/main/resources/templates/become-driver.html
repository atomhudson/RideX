Share Your Ride

Why Share Your Ride?

  • Split your travel costs with fellow commuters
  • Reduce your carbon footprint
  • Make your daily commute more enjoyable
  • Help reduce traffic congestion
  • Meet new people in your community

How It Works

1. Plan Your Trip

Enter your regular route or one-time trip details

2. Set Your Preferences

Choose your preferred pickup points and number of passengers

3. Share Your Ride

Post your trip and wait for passengers to join

4. Connect with Passengers

Review and accept passenger requests

5. Enjoy the Ride

Share your journey and split the costs
